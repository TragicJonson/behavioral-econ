##### Useful for: 

- [[Analysis Paralysis, Decision Fatigue]] 

##### Approach

1. Decide what aspect of a decision is most important
2. Establish a cutoff level
3. Eliminate all alternatives that do not meed that level
4. Repeat for every attribute until the set is narrow enough for a [[Compensatory Strategy]] evaluation
